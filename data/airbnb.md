## About Dataset

How is Airbnb really being used in and affecting the neighbourhoods of your city?
Airbnb claims to be part of the "sharing economy" and disrupting the hotel industry. However, data shows that the majority of Airbnb listings in most cities are entire homes, many of which are rented all year round - disrupting housing and communities.

Browse the data for your city below, and see for yourself.

[AirBNB Data](https://insideairbnb.com/get-the-data/)

**Worth Noting:** 
* This is a more advanced dataset to use!
* The `.csv.gz` files are more detailed than the `.csv` files. You can use the `csv` files, but recommend at least looking at the `.csv.gz` files before choosing which ones to look at.
* We have not covered how to handle text data, so the reviews datasets may be challenging for you to use. If you're interested in that, please contact your instructor to learn how to handle text data.
* The `.geojson` file provides a map of the city, which can make some great visualizations! Look up the Python [folium](https://python-visualization.github.io/folium/latest/) library for how to plot on maps.
